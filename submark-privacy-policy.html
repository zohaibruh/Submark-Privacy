Privacy Policy for Submark

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Submark ("the app") is a wordmark and logo generator developed by Zohaibahd ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the app handles your information. In short: Submark does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. Submark does not collect, request, or transmit any personal or device information. It has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. The app does not require or use an internet connection, and no data ever leaves your device.

Images you create

Everything you make in Submark is generated and processed entirely on your device. When you tap Save, the app writes the image you created to your device's photo gallery. Submark does not read, access, upload, or analyse any of your existing photos, files, or media — it only adds the images you explicitly choose to export.

Sharing

When you use the share button, Submark hands the image you created to your device's standard system share sheet. What happens next is controlled by you and the app you choose to share to. Submark itself does not send, receive, or retain anything.

Third parties

Submark does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services, and does not share information with any third party.

Children

Submark is suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
